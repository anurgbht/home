History of Ancient India

A visual history of the chronology of events and people in Ancient India. Most of the information is gathered while reading R.S.Sharma's great book India's Ancient Past

3000 BC

Writing enters the Indus culture.

1500 BC

Dravidian and non-Sanskritic terms found in Vedic texts.

1500 BC

Rig Veda.

1000 BC

Yajur Veda, Atharva Veda, the Brahmanas, Aranyakas, and the Upanishads.

300 BC

Sangam Literature

300 BC

Prakrit as the lingua franca.

400 CE

Sanskrit as the state language.

1765 CE

Bihar and Bengal came under the rule of the East India Company.
